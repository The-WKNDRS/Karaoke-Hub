About Us

Our Mission

Karaoke Hub is a veteran owned and operated web application that allows users to find karaoke venues in their area. Users can also create an account and add their favorite karaoke venues to their profile. This allows users to keep track of their favorite karaoke venues and find new ones to attend. We hope you enjoy using this website as much as we enjoyed making it.
